Vinny's Trees


    Here are some nice trees in the world:

  • Weeping Willow Trees
  • Joshua Trees
  • Wisteria Trees


    • Weeping Willow trees are native to China, but now are everywhere and are nice to sit under.



    Joshua trees can only be found at certain elevations, and are native to the southwestern US. They take many years to grow.



    Wisteria trees really enjoy sunlight and are quick to grow but can be challenging to bloom.
